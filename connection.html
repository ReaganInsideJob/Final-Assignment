Forming Social Connections

The transition from a state of chronic online presence to reclaiming control over your time takes a period of adjustment. During this transition, consider implementing the following tips:

Understand that you're not alone in feeling this way; many others share similar feelings online. Explore online forums where individuals experiencing loneliness can connect:

A Lonely Life Mental Health Forum SAMHSA Meet Up ADAA

While online interactions provide some connection, remember that physical interactions have greater benefits. As you gradually reintegrate into social settings, connect with those closest to you, such as friends, family, or even a therapist.

Developing a hobby can be a big step in reducing online time and cultivating a sense of fulfillment. Engaging in activities you enjoy will make you feel more satisfied in life compared to the time spent online.
